The Law

Don't get pulled over by these guys! Campy adventures will ensue. Here are the laws you need to be aware of to ride a motorcycle in Washington:

  • RCW 46.20.505 - Special endorsement fees
  • WAC 204-10-026 - Eye protection requirements
  • RCW 46.37.530 - Motorcycles, motor-driven cycles, mopeds, electric-assisted bicycles, helmets, other equipment - children - rules
  • RCW 46.04.330 - Motorcycle definition
  • RCW 46.20.500 - Motorcycle endorsement exceptions
  • RCW 46.20.510 - Motorcycle instruction permit
  • RCW 46.20.515 - Motorcycle skills testing
